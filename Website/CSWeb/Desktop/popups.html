Money Back Guarantee

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, return it within 60 days of the original shipment date listed on the packing slip. We will gladly refund or credit the product purchase price to your original form of payment.

To make a return, contact our Customer Service Center for a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and include this number on the outside of your return package. Returns sent with an expired RMA number or no RMA number may be refused and/or replacements could be delayed. Please pack the items securely; all parts and instruction manuals must be included. Please include a description of the defect, if applicable, and proof of purchase. Shipping, handling, and preview/trial fees are not refundable.
